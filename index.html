Research & Development

ION Performance Labs focuses on the science of building better endurance fuel.

Our goal is to ensure high-performance nutrition actually tastes good and feels right, so athletes can keep fueling easily even during their longest, toughest efforts.

Development Briefs

High-Salt Electrolytes

  • The Science 1000mg Sodium per pack. Formulated for heavy sweating in hot and humid tropical weather. Zero added sugar.
  • The Experience Salt is difficult to drink in high amounts. We use refreshing flavors like Pink Guava and Shikanji to make hydration easier to maintain.

Energy Hydrogel

  • The Science 30g Carbs (Isomaltulose). Designed for steady energy without the sugar crash or stomach pain.
  • The Experience We've created a thin, water-like texture and light flavors to prevent nausea during 10+ hour races.
